**Architecture:** Gemma 2 (Dense Decoder)
**Shape:** The "Obsidian Monolith"
**Concept:** Knowledge Compression

A highly optimized 2B parameter model. Dense, efficient, and surprisingly capable for its size.


### History
Released by Google DeepMind in **2024**. Built from the same research and technology as Gemini. It represents a shift towards high-performance open weights for the community.
